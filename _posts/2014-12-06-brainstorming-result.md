## Main Goal

_Manage your space ship and your crew and gain money for retirement_

## End of the Game

The Game ends when ...

* you reach a specific age to retire
* you lose your ship
* you are out of money

## Features
* Hire a Crew
* Send crew on missions
* Trading
* Visit different planets
* Random planet generation

## Screen content
* Ship plan
* Crew List
* Star Map
* Money Overview
